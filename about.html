About Me

My name is Darryl Milledge, a Florida A&M University Alumni and a Jacksonville, Florida native. I've pursued my real estate license to assist individuals in finding their dream homes and build a flourishing career in the commercial real estate industry.

My mission is to assist those who need financial assistance and guidance, and offer services to buyers, sellers, and investors. My values include service, hard work, integrity, new ideas, and excellence.

During my leisure time, I enjoy bowling, expanding my culinary palette, exploring, and spending time with my family. I stay connected and like to give back to my community through volunteering with my church.

My goal is to make this process enjoyable and simplified for first-time homeowners and be your trusted real estate source.
